Ars Poetica

By Monica Gomery

Whatever it was
I thought the end of my twenties
would amount to, I was wrong
about that. The end
of my twenties are about
death and the way death drapes
itself sparkling over our lives.
People are falling away
from us, people are peeling
and tumbling away.
